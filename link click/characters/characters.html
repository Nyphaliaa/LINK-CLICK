QIAO LING

Qiao Ling (Chinese: 乔苓 Qiáo Líng) is the tritagonist of the Link Click donghua series. She is the landlady of the Time Photo Studio and a close friends to its owners Cheng Xiaoshi and Lu Guang, watching over the two as they pay off their debt to her.
